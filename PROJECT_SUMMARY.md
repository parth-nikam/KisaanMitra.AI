## 🎯 Key Highlights

### Problem Solved
- **140M+ Indian farmers** losing ₹2 lakh crores annually
- 40% crop losses, 20-30% input cost waste, 25% lower market prices
- Limited access to expert advice, government schemes

### Solution
- **WhatsApp-based** (500M+ users, zero friction)
- **3 AI Agents**: Crop (disease detection), Finance (budget/schemes), Market (price forecasting)
- **Hyper-Local**: Village-level knowledge graph (600K villages)
- **Hindi-First**: MVP in Hindi, 11 languages in future
- **Voice Support**: For low-literacy farmers

### Technical Excellence
- **Cloud-Native AWS**: ECS Fargate, Lambda, SageMaker
- **ML Models**: 95%+ disease detection, 85%+ price forecasting
- **Performance**: <2s response, 99.9% uptime
- **Scalability**: 100K → 10M+ users
- **Cost**: ₹5/user/month ($0.07/user/month)

### Expected Impact
- **+20-30%** farmer income
- **-40%** crop losses
- **-15-20%** input costs
- **+25%** better market prices
- **10x** scheme access
