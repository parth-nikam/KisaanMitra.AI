### 6. 🔍 Crop Health Query (Text-Based)
```
Command: "My tomato leaves are turning yellow"
OR: "Wheat me daag aa rahe hain"
OR: "Cotton plant wilting problem"
```

**Sample Response:**
```
🌿 Crop Health Analysis - Tomato

🔍 Symptoms Identified:
• Yellowing leaves
• Possible nutrient deficiency or disease

🎯 Likely Causes:

1. Nitrogen Deficiency (Most Likely)
   • Lower leaves turn yellow first
   • Stunted growth
   • Solution: Apply urea 50kg/acre

2. Early Blight Disease
   • Yellow spots with dark centers
   • Affects older leaves first
   • Solution: Spray Mancozeb 2.5g/liter

3. Overwatering
   • Yellowing with wilting
   • Root rot possible
   • Solution: Reduce watering, improve drainage

💊 Immediate Action:
1. Check soil moisture - reduce watering if soggy
2. Apply urea fertilizer (50kg/acre)
3. If spots appear, spray fungicide immediately

📸 For accurate diagnosis, please send a clear photo of:
• Affected leaves (close-up)
• Whole plant
• Any spots or discoloration

💡 Prevention Tips:
• Maintain proper spacing (60cm x 45cm)
• Apply balanced NPK fertilizer
• Ensure good drainage
• Regular monitoring
```
