Crop Agent

Village-level intelligence for real-time crop health, disease detection, and season-specific guidance.

  • Hyper-local crop advice via village knowledge graph
  • Disease & pest diagnosis from images
  • Fertilizer & pesticide recommendations
  • Soil & weather-aware suggestions
  • Best practices from nearby successful farmers
📈
Market Agent

Data-driven demand forecasting and price intelligence to maximize selling income.

  • Demand & price forecasting (time-series AI)
  • Crop recommendation before planting
  • Nearby mandi price trends

  • Lowest price input comparison

  • Season-end ROI & profit analysis
Process Flow

Complete Agricultural Journey

From a simple "Hi" on WhatsApp to profit in the bank — our agents guide farmers through every stage.

💬
Onboarding
Farmer sends "Hi" on WhatsApp. AI collects village, crop, sowing date, irrigation type, and budget range. System creates personalized Farmer Profile + Village Knowledge Graph.
One-time setup · 2 minutes
🌱
Pre-Planting Intelligence
Market Agent analyzes demand trends to recommend the most profitable crop. Finance Agent creates full budget plan and matches government schemes. Crop Agent advises on soil prep and seed variety.
"Soybean → 18% higher margin this season"
🌾
Growing Season Support
Weekly advisories on weather, pests, irrigation, and fertilizer schedules. Real-time disease diagnosis via photo. Cost optimization by comparing local input prices and checking subsidy availability.
"Agli baarish kab aa rahi hai?"
✂️
Harvest Decision
Market Agent analyzes mandi price trends and predicts 2–4 week price movement. Recommends best nearby mandi. Advises: harvest now or wait for better prices.
"Kab harvest karu?"
💰
Selling & Season Close
Market Agent guides where to sell — mandi or private buyer — with estimated price range. Finance Agent delivers final profit calculation, ROI analysis, and recommendations for the next cycle.
Profit in bank · Season complete

Business Model

Why This Model Works

"We don't just build an AI tool — we build India's first village-level agricultural data infrastructure."

🛡️
Data Moat
Village-level agricultural data doesn't exist in India today. Every farmer interaction generates hyper-local data no competitor can replicate.
⚡
Every Rec = Revenue
AI agents deliver recommendations at the exact moment of farmer need — high-conversion touchpoints for input brands and financial partners.
📉
Zero Marginal Cost
Adding the next village costs only one field agent (~₹10–12K/month). AI and data pipelines scale at near-zero marginal cost.
🔄
Network Flywheel
More farmers → richer data → better AI → more partners → better services → more farmers. A self-reinforcing loop that's hard to disrupt.
