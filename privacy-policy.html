Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025  ·  Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Summary: KisaanMitra.AI is a WhatsApp-based AI assistant for farmers. We collect only the information necessary to provide personalized agricultural guidance. We do not sell your personal data. All data is stored securely on AWS infrastructure based in India.

1. Who We Are

KisaanMitra.AI ("we," "us," or "our") is an AI-powered agricultural advisory platform built to support Indian farmers with crop guidance, market intelligence, and financial planning. Our service is delivered via WhatsApp and is accessible to farmers across India.

Contact: hello@kisaanmitra.ai | Team KisaanMitra.AI, India

2. Information We Collect

When you interact with KisaanMitra.AI through WhatsApp, we may collect the following information:

  • Phone Number: Your WhatsApp phone number, used to identify your account and send responses.
  • Location Information: Village name, district, and state you provide to us to offer hyper-local advice.
  • Farming Information: Crop type, sowing dates, irrigation type, land size, and budget range you share with us.
  • Images: Photos of crops, pests, or diseases you send for diagnosis — stored temporarily for processing.
  • Voice Messages: Audio inputs transcribed using AWS Transcribe for processing; audio is not stored permanently.
  • Conversation History: Chat messages exchanged with our AI agents to provide context-aware responses.
  • Usage Data: Interaction patterns and feature usage to improve our AI models and service quality.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to provide and improve the KisaanMitra.AI service:

  • To provide personalized crop, market, and financial advisory specific to your village and farming context.
  • To build and improve village-level knowledge graphs that benefit all farmers in your area.
  • To send proactive alerts (price notifications, weather alerts, pest warnings) that you have opted into.
  • To match you with relevant government schemes, subsidies, and financial products.
  • To improve our AI models and recommendation quality over time.
  • To conduct research and analytics on agricultural trends (using aggregated, anonymized data only).

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: AWS (cloud infrastructure, AI, storage), Meta/WhatsApp (messaging delivery). These providers are bound by strict data processing agreements.
  • Government Agencies: Only when required by law or to process your applications for government schemes (with your explicit consent).
  • Aggregated Data: We may share anonymized, aggregated agricultural data with agri-input companies, insurers, and research institutions — this data cannot be used to identify you personally.
  • Business Partners: With your explicit consent, we may share your contact details with mandis, aggregators, or financial institutions you choose to connect with through our platform.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Amazon Web Services (AWS) infrastructure located in India. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.2+)
  • Access controls and role-based permissions for our team members
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Session data stored in Redis (ElastiCache) with automatic expiry

6. Data Retention

  • Farmer Profile & Farming Data: Retained for as long as you use the service, or until you request deletion.
  • Conversation History: Retained for 12 months to provide context-aware advice.
  • Images (crop photos): Processed and retained for 90 days, then deleted.
  • Voice messages: Transcribed immediately and original audio deleted within 24 hours.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights with respect to your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Opt-out: Opt out of non-essential notifications and data sharing.
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, send a message on WhatsApp saying "Delete my data" or email us at privacy@kisaanmitra.ai.

8. WhatsApp and Meta

Our service is delivered via WhatsApp, which is operated by Meta Platforms, Inc. By using KisaanMitra.AI on WhatsApp, you are also subject to WhatsApp's Terms of Service and Meta's Privacy Policy. We are an independent business using the WhatsApp Business API — Meta does not have access to the agricultural content of your conversations with us beyond what their platform processes for message delivery.

9. Children's Privacy

KisaanMitra.AI is intended for use by farmers who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect information from persons under 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately for deletion.

10. Cookies and Tracking

Our WhatsApp-based service does not use cookies. If you access any KisaanMitra.AI web pages, we may use minimal analytics tracking (e.g., page visit counts) through privacy-respecting tools. We do not use cross-site tracking or third-party advertising cookies.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you via WhatsApp message at least 7 days before the changes take effect. Your continued use of the service after that date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
